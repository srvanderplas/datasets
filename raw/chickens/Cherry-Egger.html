Description

Cherry Egger™ chickens are a Rhode Island Red breed. We pick out some of the darker reds and ship them to you. They are good egg laying production chicken which will lay large brown eggs and one of the best chickens for backyards. They are well known for best chickens for eggs and kids. The Cherry Egger™ chicken makes a great pick for raising backyard chickens. Cherry Egger™ chickens do very well in the winter as long as you have a wind break and dry chicken house or chicken coop. See more information on our Cackle Hatchery® website for chicken care sheets on how to raise your own chickens or other pages for great poultry for sale.
